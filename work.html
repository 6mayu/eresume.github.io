Uncle Bob

Uncle Bob's, a renowned fast food chain in Sabah, is celebrated for its delicious, crispy fried chicken prepared fresh daily. Catering to a diverse customer base, the brand offers a variety of on-the-go meals and family-sized portions. Over the years, Uncle Bob's has expanded its menu and locations, solidifying its reputation as a well-recognized name in the fast food industry.

My Job Scope

During my one-month tenure at Uncle Bob's in Tuaran, Sabah, I gained valuable experience in food preparation and customer service. Started at 10 AM to 9 PM, I prepped and deep-fried chicken, managed cashier duties, handled transactions, and provided exceptional customer service. This experience honed my ability to work efficiently under pressure and maintain high standards, which will be beneficial in any fast-paced, customer-oriented environment.

Dropshipping Business - Hijab Sales

I managed a successful dropshipping business selling high-quality hijabs, sourced from reliable suppliers. My key responsibilities included promoting products on social media through targeted marketing strategies to reach a broader audience, ensuring timely delivery, and maintaining strong supplier relationships. Engaging with customers and maintaining a robust online presence were crucial to boosting sales and customer satisfaction. This experience honed my skills in e-commerce, digital marketing, and customer service.
